# Sanity treatment in 2348

Sanity treatment — the management of psychological
trauma, mental illness, and the specific
psychological consequences of the campaign's
cosmic-horror exposures — has a substantial
contemporary toolkit but specific *limitations*
that the campaign deliberately preserves. This
entry covers what's available, what works, and
what doesn't.

## Contemporary mental-health toolkit

### Pharmaceutical management

The dominant approach for most conditions:

- **Antidepressants** — substantial; multiple
  classes; well-tolerated; effective for most
  cases of clinical depression
- **Anti-anxiety medications** — substantial
  variety; substantial effect for most cases
  of clinical anxiety
- **Antipsychotic medications** — substantial;
  effective for most cases of psychotic
  disorder
- **Mood stabilizers** — substantial; effective
  for most cases of bipolar disorder
- **Sleep medications** — substantial; for
  the major sleep disorders
- **Specialty medications** — for specific
  conditions

About 22% of UN-citizen adults have a current
mental-health prescription. The off-world rate is
substantially lower (Belters and Martians are
prescribed mental-health medications less
frequently than Earthers).

### Psychotherapy

Universal access in advanced economies:

- **Cognitive-behavioral therapy** — substantial
  evidence base; effective for many conditions
- **Psychodynamic therapy** — substantial
  practice; specific applications
- **Trauma-focused therapy** — substantial
  capability for processing specific
  traumatic experiences
- **Existential therapy** — substantial
  practice; particularly relevant for
  Earther-melancholy-related concerns
- **Family-and-couples therapy** — substantial
  practice

### Contemplative practices

The major non-pharmacological approach:

- **Lunar Temple Buddhist meditation** —
  substantial cultural penetration; the
  *seated minute* is widespread; longer
  meditative practices (15-30 minutes daily)
  are common
- **Other meditative traditions** — substantial
  practice across multiple religious traditions
- **Mindfulness-based stress reduction** — a
  secular adaptation of Buddhist practice;
  substantial clinical use
- **Body-based contemplative practices** —
  yoga and equivalent traditions; substantial

### Lifestyle interventions

- **Exercise** — substantial; documented
  effect on mental-health outcomes
- **Sleep hygiene** — substantial education
  and clinical attention
- **Social connection** — substantial
  attention to maintaining social
  relationships
- **Time-in-nature** — substantial; even
  off-world environments have substantial
  attention to recreating natural-environment
  effects

## Specialty applications

### Sleep medicine

Substantial. Most sleep disorders are
treatable. Spaceflight-specific sleep medicine is
a developed specialty.

### Anxiety disorders

Substantially treatable. Most anxiety disorders
respond well to combined pharmacological and
psychotherapeutic treatment.

### Depression

Substantially treatable. The major exception:
*reactive depression* in response to specific
overwhelming events (loss, trauma) requires
specific approaches that vary by case.

### Bipolar disorder

Substantially manageable through medication;
psychotherapy supplements pharmacological
treatment.

### Schizophrenia and related conditions

Substantially manageable; most patients can
maintain substantial functional life with
appropriate medication and support.

### Substance-use disorders

Substantial treatment options including:
medication-assisted treatment, behavioral
therapy, social support. Outcomes have improved
substantially since 2025.

### Trauma and PTSD

Substantial treatment options including:
specific PTSD-focused psychotherapies, certain
pharmacological supports, contemplative
approaches. Outcomes are generally good but
*not perfect*.

## Limits

### What can't be fully treated

- **Severe ongoing psychotic conditions** in
  some patients
- **Some severe personality disorders** —
  treatment is partial
- **Some neurodegenerative conditions** —
  treatment manages symptoms; doesn't reverse
  underlying disease
- **Acute psychotic episodes** in some
  patients — emergency intervention is
  effective; long-term management is variable
- **Specific cosmic-horror trauma** — see
  below

### What contemporary medicine can't address

- **Existential-cosmological trauma** — exposure
  to genuine cosmic horror produces effects
  that contemporary mental-health treatment
  doesn't fully address. The 2025-derived
  framework assumes a baseline reality that
  cosmic-horror exposure violates.
- **The specific Mi-go-and-related-entity
  exposure** — encounters with non-human
  intelligence of the kind the campaign
  features produce specific psychological
  effects that medicine doesn't have
  established protocols for.
- **The Caretaker-knowledge effect** — if
  the campaign reaches the late-act beats,
  the *information* the Caretakers may
  share has psychological consequences that
  contemporary therapy doesn't quite address.

## Sanity in the *Halyard* campaign

The campaign's House Rules establish a Sanity
mechanic with specific contemporary-treatment
options:

### Standard treatment

- **Pharmaceutical management** during the
  long transit — the *Halyard* carries
  substantial pharmaceutical inventory
- **Telemedicine consultation** with inner-
  system specialists (with substantial light-
  speed delay)
- **Onboard psychotherapy** through the
  Medical Officer (limited but real)
- **Father Patel's chaplaincy support** —
  pastoral counseling for crew of any
  tradition
- **Lunar Temple-style contemplative
  practices** — most crew members have some
  exposure to these and may use them during
  the long transit

### Specific Sanity recovery options

- **Ordinary stress** — restoration through
  rest, exercise, social connection
- **Significant Sanity loss** — typically
  managed through a combination of medication,
  therapy, and contemplative practice
- **Severe Sanity loss** — requires
  substantial intervention; some recovery
  possible; full recovery uncertain

### What treatment does NOT do

- **Cannot reverse cosmic-horror
  understanding** — once a PC has *seen*
  what the universe is, treatment can manage
  the response but cannot un-see the seeing
- **Cannot restore lost Sanity wholly** —
  some Sanity loss is permanent; treatment
  manages but does not fully restore
- **Cannot treat the *Caretaker-knowledge
  effect*** — if PCs accept the Request and
  return changed, treatment can support but
  not undo

## The Halyard's mental-health resources

### Standard ship resources

- **Comprehensive pharmaceutical inventory**
  — including most of the major mental-
  health categories
- **Medical Officer mental-health
  capability** — the Officer has substantial
  training in basic clinical psychiatry
- **Father Patel** — pastoral support for
  crew of any tradition
- **Telemedicine** — for substantial
  consultation, with light-speed delay
- **Routine wellness practices** — exercise
  facilities, contemplative-practice
  resources, social-activity infrastructure

### Specialized capability for severe cases

- **Acute psychiatric intervention** — the
  Medical Officer can handle most acute
  presentations
- **Pharmaceutical adjustment** — for
  ongoing medication management
- **Crisis psychotherapy** — limited but
  real
- **Specialized consultation** — through
  telemedicine; substantial light-speed
  delay limits real-time consultation

## Notes for play

- **Sanity loss is *real*** in the campaign.
  Players should expect their PCs to lose
  Sanity from cosmic-horror exposures.
- **Treatment is substantial** but *not
  perfect*. PCs can recover substantially
  from Sanity loss but typically not
  completely.
- **The *Halyard* has resources** for
  routine and substantial mental-health
  care. Players should engage with these
  resources during the long transit; not
  using them is a character choice with
  consequences.
- **The Caretaker-knowledge effect** is the
  campaign's *upper limit* on what
  contemporary treatment can address.
  Players who reach those late-act beats
  should know that treatment will support,
  not erase, the changed PCs.
