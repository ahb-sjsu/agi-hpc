# Hygiea

Hygiea is the fourth-largest body in the asteroid Belt
and the most *residentially* oriented of the major Belt
locations. Its 5 million residents work a body whose
economy is diversified between mining, agricultural
production (substantial greenhouse-and-hydroponic
operations), and specialty manufacturing — none
dominant, all reasonably productive.

The body's character is *residential-Belt*. Hygiea is
where Belters who don't want Vesta's industrial
intensity, Pallas's reserve-adjacent restrictions, or
Ceres's metropolitan scale tend to settle. The body's
political register is *moderate, family-oriented,
cooperative*.

## The body

- Diameter: 434 km
- Surface gravity: 0.018 g
- Composition: carbonaceous chondrite (the largest
  C-type body in the Belt)
- Distance from Sun: 3.14 AU (varies)

The carbonaceous composition is the body's principal
economic asset. Hygiea provides the largest single
source of carbon-rich and water-rich raw materials in
the Belt; the agricultural and biological-supply
industries depend substantially on Hygiea-sourced
material.

## Settlement

Hygiea's 5 million residents are concentrated in:

- **Hygiea Capital** (2M) — the largest single city,
  sub-surface tunnel city
- **Spin-habitat communities** (1.5M) — several
  medium-scale spin habitats with populations from
  100K-400K each
- **Surface-and-near-surface settlements** (1.2M) —
  smaller communities including the agricultural-
  greenhouse complexes
- **Smaller residential zones** (0.3M)

## Politics

Hygiea's politics are *centrist-cooperative*. The body
has been one of the OPA Plenum's most reliably
moderate constituencies; the Cunha Caucus has
substantial Hygiean representation.

The current Provincial Governor is Adisa Patel-
Mendoza. The political register is *family-and-
community oriented* with a particular emphasis on
education and cultural-development funding.

## Economy

Hygiea's economy is roughly $0.3T UN-equivalent.
Sectors:

- **Mining and extraction** — carbon-and-water-rich
  ore production
- **Agriculture and food production** — substantial;
  Hygiea supplies a meaningful fraction of inner-
  Belt fresh produce
- **Specialty manufacturing** — small-scale,
  high-margin
- **Higher education** — the University of Hygiea
  has a respected agricultural-and-biological-
  sciences program

## Cultural texture

Hygiea's cultural register is *quiet, family-oriented,
agricultural-aware*. The body is less politically and
culturally prominent than other major Belt
locations; this is, by widespread Hygiean assessment,
the way they prefer it.

## Notes for play

- A Hygiean PC has a *moderate, family-oriented*
  register that distinguishes them from the more
  politically active Belt PC options.
- The agricultural background is natural; Hygiean
  PCs default to comfortable familiarity with food
  production.
