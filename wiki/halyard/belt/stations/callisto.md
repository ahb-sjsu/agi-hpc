# Callisto

Callisto is the second-largest population center in the
Jovian system after Ganymede, the principal mining-and-
refining world of the outer planets, and the most
*industrially-pure* of the major Belt bodies. Its 9
million residents work the moon's substantial volatile-
and-mineral resources or operate the refining-and-
processing complexes that feed the Jovian-system
manufacturing economy.

Callisto's character is *industrial-technical, working-
class, OPA-Frontier-aligned*. The body's politics
align with Vesta more than with Ganymede — labor-
oriented, suspicious of inner-system arrangements, and
firmly committed to OPA institutional autonomy.

## The body

- Diameter: 4,821 km (the third-largest moon in the
  solar system)
- Surface gravity: 0.126 g
- Surface temperature: -139°C (mean)
- Composition: half rock, half water-ice; substantial
  sub-surface ocean (less geologically active than
  Europa's; the ocean is *deeper* and possibly more
  ice-isolated)
- Distance from Sun: 5.2 AU (Jupiter system)
- Magnetosphere: outside Ganymede's magnetic
  protection; substantial Jovian radiation exposure

Callisto's distance from Jupiter and consequent
exposure to lower magnetospheric protection make
surface operations *hazardous* in a different way
than the inner Jovian moons. Settlement is heavily
sub-surface; surface operations require extensive
shielding.

## Settlement structure

Callisto's 9 million residents are concentrated:

- **Asgard City** (4M) — the planetary capital;
  sub-surface tunnel city in the Asgard Crater
  region. Provincial Council, the Callistan
  Refinery Complex, the principal commercial
  center.
- **Valhalla Industrial Zone** (2.5M) — the
  largest single industrial complex outside the
  inner system. The Valhalla Refining Authority
  handles substantial fractions of Jovian-system
  metallic-ore processing.
- **Surface-Adjacent Operations** (1M) — mining-and-
  extraction operations near the surface (with
  substantial radiation exposure; rotation
  schedules are strict).
- **Orbital Habitats** (1M) — orbital
  installations in Jupiter's magnetic shadow,
  providing alternative residential options for
  workers who can manage orbital life.
- **Smaller settlements** (0.5M).

## Politics

Callisto's politics are *labor-OPA*. The Asteroid
Miners' Federation has substantial Callistan
membership; the OPA Plenum's Frontier League faction
has its strongest Jovian-system constituency on
Callisto.

The current Provincial Governor is Marcus Volkov-
Brennt, an Asteroid Miners' Federation veteran.
Volkov-Brennt's political register is *firmly pro-
labor, suspicious of Inner-System cooperation,
committed to OPA institutional independence*.

## Economy

Callisto's economy is roughly $0.6T UN-equivalent.
Sectors:

- **Mining and ore extraction** — substantial; both
  Callistan ores and processing of imported
  asteroid-belt material
- **Refining and metallurgy** — the dominant sector;
  the Valhalla complex is one of the major
  metallurgical operations in human space
- **Manufacturing** — secondary; mostly heavy-
  industrial components
- **Logistics** — Callisto is the Jovian system's
  principal industrial-trade hub

## Cultural texture

Callisto's cultural register is *industrial-Jovian*:

- **The mining-refining register.** Callistan
  cultural production centers on the work — physical
  labor, refining processes, the dangers of
  industrial accidents, the camaraderie of crew
  work. The Callistan literary tradition is heavy on
  workers' fiction.
- **The radiation-aware register.** Callistan
  outdoor activity is constrained by radiation
  exposure; the cultural production engages with
  *time outside* as a measured commodity.
- **The Jovian-aware register.** Jupiter dominates
  the sky from Callisto more dramatically than from
  Ganymede (Callisto's larger orbital distance
  produces a different visual perspective). The
  cultural relationship with Jupiter is intense.
- **The OPA-political register.** Labor politics is
  a constant cultural reference. Most Callistans
  are politically engaged; the cultural production
  reflects active political life.

## Notes for play

- A Callistan PC will be more *industrial-labor-OPA*
  than other Belt-PC options.
- The mining-refining background is natural; many
  Callistan PCs have metallurgical or extraction
  expertise.
- The radiation-management register is a useful
  texture for outer-system PCs.
