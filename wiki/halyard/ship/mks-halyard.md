The **MKS *Halyard*** is a **Lambert-class deep-range science
vessel** chartered to Mao–Kwikowski Mercantile's Outer-System
Science Division. Registry: Luna. Transponder reads current
name only.

## Specifications

- **Class:** Lambert-class (Lambert & Wei Engineering, Luna).
- **Length:** 140 m.
- **Dry mass:** 6 kilotonnes.
- **Drive:** Epstein continuous. 0.3 g cruise standard; can push
  to 1.4 g sustained.
- **Endurance:** 15–18 months full complement (12). This run
  flies lean at 7–9 months onsite-plus-transit with a crew
  of ~11.
- **Spin-grav hab ring:** 0.4 g nominal at the outer surface.
  A persistent 0.32 g harmonic (mess + corner of the gym) has
  never been engineered out; Chief Engineer Priya Desai tried
  three times and gave up.
- **Comms:** deep-range comms suite (2346 refit).
- **Lab:** BSL-3 containment, 2346 addition. Used twice.
- **Science suite:** geochem benches, spectroscopy, radiological
  survey, 12-place sample freezer stack, 2-place xenobio
  containment.
- **Landers:** two **Kestrel-class** short-hop chemical landers in
  dorsal cradles.
- **Weapons:** one aft-dorsal **Point Defense Cannon** listed as
  decommissioned.
