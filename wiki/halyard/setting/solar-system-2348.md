Humanity lives from Luna to the Kuiper Belt. Total population
about 23 billion, split across three broad blocs:

- **Earth & Luna — ~20 billion.** Governed by the UN; Luna is a UN
  protectorate and serves as the system's shipyard, corporate HQ,
  and sanatorium. Luna cities of note: Shackleton (south-pole
  research), Tycho (shipbuilding), Copernicus (financial &
  administrative), Aristarchus (media), Serenity Industrial (mining
  & refining), Apennine (middle-class residential).
- **Mars — 3 billion.** The Martian Congressional Republic (MCR),
  independent since 2293. Indoor population; atmosphere ~170 mbar
  after a century of terraforming; breathability projected 2650.
  Capital: Lowell City. Naval arm: MCRN, smaller than the UNN but
  tactically superior in every war-game since 2331.
- **The Belt — ~400 million.** Scattered across Ceres, Eros,
  Pallas, Vesta, Tycho Station, and the Jovian/Saturnian habs.
  Culturally distinct: Belter physiology (low-g adaptation), Lang
  Belta creole, water-dominant politics. OPA is an umbrella of
  Belter interests, not a government.

The **Epstein drive** (fusion-torch, invented 2285 by Solomon
Epstein on Tycho Station) is what makes this civilization
possible. Continuous-burn transit times:

- Luna → Mars: ~3 weeks
- Luna → Ceres: ~4–6 weeks
- Inner → Saturn: ~3–4 months
- Neptune → 80+ AU TNO: ~6 months additional

The **Outer Dark** past Neptune is sparse: ~7 UNN listening posts,
~4 MCR observation stations, ~40 commercial waystations, a dozen
long-endurance research vessels, a thousand survey drones.

**What does not exist** in 2348: faster-than-light travel, FTL
communications, artificial general intelligence in the classical
sense, and any confirmed extraterrestrial intelligence.

**Currencies:** UN credits are the default; Ares dollars on Mars
(~1.12 UN credits); Belter script informally pegged to water/air
shares. 48,000 UN credits is a year of Luna middle-class living;
a deep-range hazard contract pays ~$190k end-to-end.
