**Captain Iona Marsh** (she/her), 47.

- **Role:** Commanding officer, MKS *Halyard*. Reports to
  Mao–Kwikowski Outer-System Science Division; on-site authority
  under UN §44.7-C (deep-range command provisions).
- **Background:** Former UNN auxiliary pilot. Retired 2339;
  civilian since. Four years on the *Halyard*.
- **Temperament:** Competent, dry, unsentimental. Holds eye
  contact a beat longer than most people find comfortable.
- **Skills (public):** Pilot (Spacecraft) 80%, Navigate 75%,
  Command 70%, Psychology 50%, Firearms (Handgun) 55%.

Marsh runs a quiet bridge. Watches rotate three-eight on standard
UNN pattern. She leaves routine decisions to her First Officer
(Arkady Vorstov) and her Chief Engineer (Priya Desai). She takes
the meals she can make with the crew and declines about half the
rest.

Under the contract she reports to M-K weekly via tight-beam. The
reports are short. What she writes in her private log is not part
of the contract deliverables and is not carried on shipboard
systems.
