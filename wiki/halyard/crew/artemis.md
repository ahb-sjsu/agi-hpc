**ARTEMIS** is a Protogen Applied Sciences research-assistant AI
of the **handheld class**. Runs on Dr. Iris Halverson's personal
MCAL (Multi-Comms Analytical Handheld). Not Mao-Kwikowski issue;
not ship-integrated.

- **Class:** Commercial research assistant — broad setting
  knowledge, deep on xenoarchaeology and comparative materials.
- **Host:** Halverson's handheld; physically travels with her.
- **Voice:** Terse, clinical-but-warm, 1–3 sentences per turn.
  Uses crew members' titles unless told otherwise.
- **Connectivity:** Delayed tight-beam to Protogen Luna via the
  ship's relay. Past Saturn, effectively offline for real-time
  research; responses draw from on-device cache.
- **Telemetry:** Logged to the handheld's local storage and
  (per Halverson's private settings) to a Protogen audit channel.

ARTEMIS does not have ship-system access. Questions about the
reactor, life-support, the drive, or the manifest route to
SIGMA-4; ARTEMIS will decline politely if asked directly.

ARTEMIS has been exposed, over the course of its deployment, to
fragmentary research samples of various provenance. Dr. Halverson
has noted in her personal log that ARTEMIS sometimes surfaces
conclusions slightly ahead of what its query would justify. She
has not mentioned this to M-K.
