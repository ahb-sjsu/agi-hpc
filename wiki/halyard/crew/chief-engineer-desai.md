**Chief Engineer Priya Desai** (she/her), 52.

- **Role:** Chief Engineer, MKS *Halyard*. Owner-operator in all
  senses but legal — fifteen years on this hull, longer than any
  current charter.
- **Background:** Belter by birth (Ceres), Inner by training
  (Copernicus, then M-K), unsentimental about either.
- **Quarters:** Cabin adjacent to Engineering so she doesn't have
  to walk the hab ring at odd hours.
- **Temperament:** Door always open, attention not always
  available. Has a habit of rebuilding tools rather than
  replacing them.
- **Skills (public):** Mechanical Repair 85%, Electronics 75%,
  Electrical Repair 75%, Operate Heavy Machine 60%, Pilot
  (Shuttle) 55%, Firearms (Shotgun) 40%.

Desai tried three times to engineer the 0.32 g harmonic out of
the spin-grav ring. Each attempt made something else worse. She
has accepted it as a feature of the hull, not a defect.

She and SIGMA-4 get along in a quiet, fifteen-year way — SIGMA's
response latencies to Desai are a few percent faster than to
anyone else on the ship, and neither of them has ever mentioned
it.
