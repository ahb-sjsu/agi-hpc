The **United Nations** of 2348 is a federal government covering
Earth and Luna. Oldest continuously-functioning political
institution in the solar system.

## Structure

- **General Assembly** — legislative, representatives from every
  member nation.
- **Secretariat** — executive; Secretary-General serves a 9-year
  term, indirectly elected by the General Assembly.
- **Security Council** — reformed 2198, 9-member rotating body.
- **Court of Justice** — highest court of UN law.
- **Administrative agencies** — dozens, ranging from meteorology
  to deep-range contract oversight (UN §44.7).

## UNN — UN Navy

Largest military force in human space by tonnage:
- **~2,400** commissioned vessels across all classes.
- Home Fleet (Earth-Luna), Outer-System Fleet (Mars orbit,
  Belt, Jovian), Deep-Range Fleet.
- Signal Corps, Auxiliary (logistics, hospital, rescue),
  Special Operations.

The UNN has lost every major MCRN war-game since 2331. Numerous
but not more capable than the MCRN; built for presence rather
than war-fighting.

## UN §44.7

The regulatory framework under which outer-system hazardous
contracts operate. Every charter the *Halyard* flies under is a
§44.7 contract: oversight, audit, and certain disclosure
requirements apply.

## Relationship to the OPA

The UN recognizes the OPA as a political voice but not as a
government. OPA representatives hold observer seats on the UN
Outer-System Subcommittee.

## Signals Directorate

The UNN's signals-intelligence apparatus. Monitors outer-system
commercial traffic as a matter of routine. Not typically hostile
to contractors; occasionally recruits. Its scope and staffing
are publicly undisclosed.
