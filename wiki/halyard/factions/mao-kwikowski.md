**Mao–Kwikowski Mercantile** (M-K) is the *Halyard*'s employer.

- **Founded:** 2291 by Feng Mao and Eryk Kwikowski as a
  Mars–Earth freight concern.
- **Today:** Luna-registered (88-2204) megacorp. Headquartered on
  the 185th floor of M-K Tower, Copernicus, Luna.
- **CEO (2348):** Dhanesh Tewari — third-generation executive,
  engineering background.
- **Board:** 11 members. 6 family-affiliated (Mao and Kwikowski
  descendants), 5 professional.

## Divisions

- **Freight** — largest. Luna-Mars-Ceres-Jovian heavy shipping.
- **Heavy Industry** — shipbuilding (via subsidiaries), fusion
  reactor components, mining equipment.
- **Outer-System Science Division (OSS)** — ~40 ships, ~3,000
  personnel. The *Halyard* is one of theirs.
- **Pharmaceutical Services.**
- **Corporate Services.**

## Reputation

- Boring, reliable, patient by industry reputation.
- Pays on time. Every time.
- One public incident in the last 40 years: the 2321 Jovian
  mining-platform accident. Settled for 430M UN credits and a
  series of safety reforms that have largely held.

## Related to the *Halyard*

- Contracting officer for this mission: **D. Ritter**,
  ritter.d@mk-mercantile.lun.
- On-site authority: **Captain Iona Marsh**.
- Post-mission debrief liaison: **Associate Director Halden
  Rutherford** (Luna; crew typically has not met him).

## Relationship to Protogen

Direct rival. M-K's OSS Division competes with Protogen Applied
Sciences on outer-system contracts; their personnel know each
other, and a subset of personnel on any given mission are
thought to be assets of the other side. Industry norm.
