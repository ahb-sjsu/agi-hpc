## Contract basics

- **Employer:** Mao–Kwikowski Mercantile, Outer-System Science
  Division.
- **Registry:** Luna–Ceres.
- **Contract id:** OSS-2348-117-N. Posted 14.04.2348.
- **Muster:** Ceres Station, Berth 47-D.
- **Departure:** 28.04.2348.
- **Outbound transit:** ~184 days, Epstein-continuous, rolling
  brachistochrone.
- **Onsite:** ~90 days surface operations, extensible at the
  Lead's discretion.
- **Return:** Guaranteed, same-hull, earliest 11.2349.

## Remuneration

- **Base:** 48,000 UN credits, paid pro-rata per standard day.
- **Hazard modifier:** +220% while onsite (Tier-3: hard vacuum,
  high-rem, corrosive atmosphere).
- **Completion bonus:** 75,000 UN credits on successful sample
  return and debrief.
- **Family indemnity:** 1.2M UN credits on death, loss, or
  cognitive-grade deviation of 30% or greater.

## Requirements

- Current medical (≤60 days).
- Psychiatric clearance, Chamber-standard.
- Rad-exposure log, life-to-date.
- Next-of-kin notifications filed per UN §44.7-B prior to
  muster.
- 90-year discretion clause, signed and witnessed.
- No published work on speculative cosmology, extra-system
  anthropology, or dream-state phenomenology within the last 60
  months.

## Exclusions

- Current or former membership in religious orders specializing
  in celestial cosmology.
- Active subscribers to non-UN faith systems invoking extra-
  system origins.
- Personnel with documented exposure to Class-4 uncharacterized
  biologicals within 36 months.
- Applicants previously withdrawn from outer-system contracting
  on medical grounds not fully disclosed.

## Destination

The contract does not name the destination. What is publicly
posted: "a trans-Neptunian object past Neptune, TNO catalog
reference withheld." Crew learn the catalog designation
(TNO 2301-DR-44) at muster and the informal name (Nithon) only
after boarding.
