**Ceres** — the de facto Belter capital, roughly 8 million
permanent residents with another 2 million transient. Spun to
0.3 g by a century of continuous rotation. OPA-aligned; legally
administered under a UN protectorate with substantial Belter
autonomy.

- **Port:** Ceres Station, concentric-ring design. Berth 47-D
  is the MKS *Halyard*'s muster point.
- **Culture:** Dominant Belter culture. Lang Belta widely spoken
  alongside English. Religious mix: Orthodox Christian, Islamic,
  Hindu, Sikh communities plus the Church of Humanity Ascendant
  (Belter secular-ritualist, ~8% of adults).
- **Economy:** Transshipment hub for the outer system. Water
  refinery; mushroom-protein agriculture; outer-system rare-
  earth processing.
- **Food:** Kibble is the baseline; Ganymede produce is the
  luxury. Real meat is a birthday food.
- **OPA presence:** Strong — Anderson Dawes's Ceres Bloc
  headquarters.

The *Halyard* spent twelve days on Ceres Station before departure
for final provisioning. The crew disembark via passenger ferry
from Luna (Tycho Station); the ferry run is ~4,500 UN credits
economy, ~14,000 first class.

The ship departed Ceres under 0.3 g at 28.04.2348. Return is
scheduled to Luna, not Ceres.
